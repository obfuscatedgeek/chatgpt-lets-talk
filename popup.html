ChatGPT - Let's talk

Adds keyboard shortcuts to ChatGPT site which allows you to talk to and hear AI speak in the site.

Currently 3 actions are available, press the following combination of keys to perform the respective action.

Change voices

Vocies Pitch Rate

Keyboard shortcuts

Window Mac Action
Alt+⇧Shift+F ⌥ Option+⇧Shift+F Talk :   The extension is ready & actively listening, ask your question using your voice / microphone.
Alt+⇧Shift+J ⌥ Option+⇧Shift+J Hear :  Although the extension will read the answer out loud once it's finished generating it, by pressing these keys, the last answer will be read again.
Alt+⇧ Shift+P ⌥ Option+⇧Shift+P Stop :  If pressed while the answer is being read, the extension will stop reading.
